青蟹 Scylla

青蟹属广泛分布于太平洋、印度洋，其分布区城西到印度洋的南非海岸和红海，南到澳大利亚、新西南海域，东到太平洋的夏咸夷和塔西提岛。 青蟹是广温性底栖动物，其适温范围在7-35℃；同时是穴居动物，白天多居于穴中，夜间出来觅食。爬行时靠步足，游泳时全靠游泳足运动， 以软体动物、动物尸体、少量藻类为食。繁殖期为5-9月，卵化的幼体漂于潮间带觅食、生长。

The genus Green Crab is widely distributed in the Pacific and Indian Oceans, ranging from the west coast of South Africa and the Red Sea in the Indian Ocean, south to Australia and the New Southwest Sea, and east to the Xia Xianyi and Tahiti islands in the Pacific Ocean. Green crabs are warm benthic animals with a suitable temperature range of 7-35 ℃; They are also cave dwelling animals, mostly residing in their burrows during the day and coming out to forage at night. When crawling, it relies on walking feet, and when swimming, it relies entirely on swimming feet for movement, feeding on mollusks, animal carcasses, and a small amount of algae. The breeding period is from May to September, and the egg shaped larvae float in the intertidal zone to forage and grow.

海龟 Chelonia spp

海龟是世界上最古老的动物之一，大多生活在比较浅的沿海水域、海湾、珊瑚礁和流入大海的河口等地方，以大叶藻、头足类、甲壳类及鱼虾为食。幼龟自出壳即爬归海水中生活。海龟在成熟之前，雄性和雌性的体态是一样的，当雄性海龟成熟时，尾巴就会变长，达体长的1/2。

Turtles are one of the oldest animals in the world, mostly living in shallow coastal waters, bays, coral reefs, and estuaries that flow into the ocean. They feed on large algae, cephalopods, crustaceans, and fish and shrimp. Young turtles crawl back into the sea to live as soon as they hatch. Before maturity, the body shape of males and females in sea turtles is the same. When males mature, their tails become longer, reaching half of their body length.

鲸鱼 whale

鲸鱼是海洋中最大的生物，也是世界上最古老的海洋哺乳动物之一。它们主要生活在各种海洋环境中，从深海到近海，从温暖的热带水域到寒冷的极地海域。鲸鱼以其庞大的体型和优雅的姿态而著称。它们是海洋生态系统中的顶级捕食者，对维持生态平衡起着重要作用。鲸鱼的繁殖行为也颇为复杂，它们通常在特定的季节和地点繁殖，雌鲸会在这个时期回到陆地的海滩上生产。

Whales are the largest creatures in the ocean and one of the oldest marine mammals in the world. They mainly live in various marine environments, from deep sea to nearshore, from warm tropical waters to cold polar waters. Whales are known for their large size and elegant posture. They are the top predators in marine ecosystems and play an important role in maintaining ecological balance. The reproductive behavior of whales is also quite complex. They usually breed in specific seasons and locations, and female whales return to the beaches on land to produce during this period.

海马 Hippocampus

海马是海龙目海龙科暖海生数种小型鱼类的统称，是一种小型海洋动物，身长5～30厘米。因头部弯曲与体近直角而得名，头呈马头状而与身体形成一个角，吻呈长管状，口小，背鳍一个，均为鳍条组成。眼可以各自独立活动。海马行动迟缓，却能很有效率地捕捉到行动迅速、善于躲藏的桡足类生物，分布在大西洋、欧洲、太平洋、澳大利亚。等珊瑚礁中。

Hippocampus is a collective term for several small warm marine fish species in the family Syngnathidae, order Syngnathiformes. It is a small marine animal with a body length of 5-30 centimeters. Named after its curved head and nearly right angle to the body, it has a horsehead shape and forms an angle with the body, a long tubular snout, a small mouth, and a dorsal fin, all composed of fins. Eyes can move independently. Hippocampus is slow to move, but can efficiently capture fast-moving and adept at hiding copepods, distributed in the Atlantic, Europe, Pacific, and Australia. 章鱼 octopus

章鱼是海洋中最为神秘的生物之一，以其独特的外形和智能而著称。它们拥有一对长长的触手，长度可达到其身体的一倍，这使得它们能够灵活地捕捉和处理猎物。章鱼的生活习性复杂多样，它们不仅以其独特的身体结构捕食，还以其出色的智能和适应能力而闻名。在求偶季节，雄性章鱼会展开激烈的竞争，以争夺雌性的青睐。它们的繁殖行为包括建造繁殖巢穴和保护卵，以确保后代的繁衍。

Octopuses are one of the most mysterious creatures in the ocean, known for their unique appearance and intelligence. They have a pair of long tentacles, which can reach twice the length of their body, allowing them to flexibly capture and process prey. Octopuses have complex and diverse living habits. They not only prey with their unique body structure, but are also known for their excellent intelligence and adaptability. During the mating season, male octopuses engage in fierce competition to compete for the favor of females. Their reproductive behavior includes building breeding nests and protecting eggs to ensure the reproduction of their offspring.

鲨鱼 shark

鲨鱼是海洋中的顶级捕食者，广泛分布于全球的温暖海域。它们的分布区域从印度洋的南非海岸和红海，延伸到澳大利亚和新西南海域，以及太平洋的夏威夷和塔西提岛。鲨鱼是典型的广温性动物，能够在摄氏7到35度的水温下生存；它们同时也是底栖生物，白天多在海底洞穴或岩石缝隙中休息，夜间则出来觅食。它们的食性多样，主要以鱼类、海豚、海龟甚至是其他鲨鱼为食。鲨鱼的繁殖期多在春末至秋初，母鲨会在体内孵化卵或直接分娩幼鲨，幼鲨在出生后会在潮间带觅食和生长。

Sharks are the top predators in the ocean, widely distributed in warm waters around the world. Their distribution ranges from the South African coast and Red Sea in the Indian Ocean, to Australia and the New Southwest Sea, as well as Hawaii and Tahiti in the Pacific Ocean. Sharks are typical temperate animals that can survive in water temperatures ranging from 7 to 35 degrees Celsius; They are also benthic organisms, often resting in underwater caves or rock crevices during the day and coming out to forage at night. Their diet is diverse, mainly feeding on fish, dolphins, sea turtles, and even other sharks. The breeding period of sharks is mostly from late spring to early autumn. Mother sharks will hatch eggs or directly give birth to young sharks. Young sharks will forage and grow in the intertidal zone after birth.

剑鱼 Chelonia spp

剑鱼是海洋中的速度王者，以其独特的外形和惊人的速度著称。这种鱼类通常栖息在较浅的沿海水域、开放的海洋以及温暖的海域，以小型鱼类和甲壳类动物为食。在成熟之前，雌性和雄性剑鱼的体态相似，然而一旦成熟，雄性剑鱼的特征就会明显突出。它们的尾巴变得异常延长，长度可达到其体长的的一半，这使得它们在水中游动时能够产生巨大的推进力。剑鱼这个名字来源于它们长而尖的的上颌，这种特征使它们能够轻易捕捉猎物。

Swordfish are the speed king in the ocean, known for their unique appearance and amazing speed. This type of fish typically inhabits shallow coastal waters, open oceans, and warm seas, feeding on small fish and crustaceans. Before maturity, the body shape of female and male swordfish is similar, but once mature, the characteristics of male swordfish become more prominent. Their tails have become abnormally elongated, reaching up to half their body length, allowing them to generate tremendous propulsion when swimming in water. The name swordfish comes from their long and pointed upper jaw, which allows them to easily capture prey.

栉水母 Comb jellies

栉水母，是栉水母动物门有触手纲球栉水母的无脊椎动物。栉水母均分布于海水生境，某些种生活于半咸水性质的环境。除少数爬行及寄生的种类外，栉水母多自由漂浮于水中。通常被风浪冲成大群，尤其是海湾、潟湖及其他沿岸水域中。栉水母属于稀缺海洋生物，对科学研究也有着十分重要的价值。

The comb jellyfish is an invertebrate of the order Actinopterygii in the phylum of comb jellyfish. The jellyfish are distributed in marine habitats, and some species live in semi saline environments. Except for a few crawling and parasitic species, the jellyfish mostly float freely in the water. Usually formed into large groups by wind and waves, especially in bays, lagoons, and other coastal waters. The jellyfish is a rare marine organism and has significant value for scientific research.
